## 🧬 **THE MECHANISM: WOBBLE POSITION AS ESCAPE ARCHITECTURE**

### **Part A: Quantum Biology of Codon Position 3**

Slocombe et al. (2022) modeled wobble mispairing by coupling first-principles quantum chemistry to open quantum systems, calculating proton transfer between bases and demonstrating that quantum tunnelling plays a critical role in wobble position mispairings, with rates increased 100-fold by tunnelling-ready configurations in the polymerase active site.

**Biological Consequence:** DNA sequence changes at codon position 3 may not change the corresponding amino acid because of wobble hypothesis redundancy; however, "silent" synonymous mutations can still impact protein synthesis and function by changing the rate of ribosomal translation, potentially folding the protein in abnormal ways.

### **Part B: The col(F) / ker(F) Partition**

The genetic code is a 64→20 surjection (64 codons → 20 amino acids). This creates two irreducible subspaces:

| Space | Dimension | Biological Role | Mutation Rate | Current Surveillance |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **col(F)** | 20 | Amino acid sequences (H, F, N, P, L, M proteins) | Low (Positions 1–2) | ✅ Explicitly optimized |
| **ker(F)** | 44 | Synonymous codon substitutions (immune evasion) | **High (Position 3)** | ❌ **Completely invisible** |

### **Part C: Why Current Vaccines Fail at Wobble Position**

Recent analysis of measles virus T-cell epitopes classified three codon positions as diversifying (with evidence of positive selection): P120 within P108–122 epitope, F419 within F409–423, and H520 within H511–525 epitope. Notably, these mutations can occur at Position 3 while preserving amino acid identity, evading antibody recognition generated against the col(F) sequence.

When measles virus escapes a specific monoclonal antibody therapy, it also escapes an epitope involved in vaccine protection, decreasing the number of additional mutations required to escape vaccine protection entirely.

### **Part D: AI's Architectural Blindness**

Current biology AI systems (AlphaFold 3, Evo 2, ESMC) operate in Euclidean geometry. Recent studies show that adaptive data embedding in hyperbolic space, rather than Euclidean, reveals that viral evolution leaves dimensionality unchanged but produces logarithmic increase in curvature under selective pressures, indicating constant rate of information acquisition optimized under vaccination.

Euclidean-based Transformer embeddings cannot encode the hierarchical asymmetry across codon positions.
